About Kimure

The AI Brokerage Layer for Real Estate, Land, and Finance

Kimure is building trusted AI infrastructure that helps buyers, sellers, investors, and institutions discover, verify, value, and transact with speed, clarity, and security.

Request Early Access Explore Platform

Our Mission

Make real estate and land transactions smarter and safer by combining verification, AI-driven insights, and finance tooling into one brokerage-grade workflow.

Why Kimure

These markets are fragmented. Data is inconsistent. Trust is expensive. Kimure brings structure to discovery, due diligence, and deal execution so decisions become faster and more reliable.

What We Build

Verification and Trust

Verified listings, document validation, identity checks, and compliance workflows.

AI Valuation and Intelligence

Pricing, confidence scoring, market signals, geospatial context, and investment scenarios.

Matching and Deal Routing

Intent-based matching across buyers, sellers, investors, lenders, and institutions.

Finance Layer

Affordability checks, lender comparisons, mortgage pathways, and ROI projections.

Who It’s For

Buyers and Sellers

Find verified opportunities, reduce risk, and move through transactions with confidence.

Investors

Compare deals using AI insights, financial projections, and trust signals.

Lenders and Institutions

Standardize inputs, streamline decisioning, and improve underwriting visibility.

Brokers and Operators

Upgrade workflows with smart due diligence, faster matching, and compliant deal execution.

How It Works

  1. Discover verified listings with AI-guided search.
  2. Validate identity, documents, and risk signals.
  3. Value with AI valuation, comparables, and market signals.
  4. Transact through secure workflows, e-sign, and tracked milestones.
  5. Finance with lender pathways and investment scenarios.

Trust by Design

  • Verified listings and standardized due diligence
  • Audit-friendly workflows and clear transaction tracking
  • Security-first data handling and role-based access patterns
  • Compliance checks and document integrity signals
